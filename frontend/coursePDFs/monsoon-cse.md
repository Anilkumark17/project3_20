Introduces qualitative and quantitative user research methods for UX design, covering interviews, surveys, usability testing, field studies, and data synthesis to inform human-centered design decisions.

### 🎯 Learning Outcomes

- Apply qualitative and quantitative research frameworks to understand user needs and behaviors.
- Design and conduct field studies, stakeholder interviews, and usability evaluations.
- Synthesize user research data to identify pain points and design opportunities.
- Develop research proposals and present findings effectively to stakeholders.
- Evaluate the ethical considerations in conducting user research studies.
